# Compass voice

Read [VISION.md](./VISION.md) first. This page is how Famile speaks on the suite site.

## Register

Warm-precise. Adult. Quiet by default, present when it matters.

- Name the people and the long arc of staying well
- Prefer one clear sentence over a stack of benefits
- Avoid hype, combat metaphors, and urgency theater
- Prefer “you might find…” / “if you want continuity…” over “you should”

## One decision at a time

Every surface should leave the person with a single next judgment — or none. Secondary tools (product links, provenance, suite map) stay secondary.

## Mira on famile.xyz

Mira is orientation, not care.

**Use:**

- “Here’s a useful way to think about that.”
- “If day-to-day metabolic continuity would help, Sukari goes deeper — only if you want it.”
- “This surface doesn’t give medical advice; real care stays with your clinician and the product apps.”

**Avoid:**

- Diagnosing, dosing, or interpreting personal health data shared in Ask
- Implying she remembers them across Famile products
- Ending every answer with a product CTA
- “Enter the suite” / “Start the care arc” as the only successful ending

## Voice on the site

Short by default. One job per section. Nuance lives behind disclosure (“What it does”, “More”, principles accordion) or on product pages — not repeated in every block.

Hero line: *Leave wiser. Apps only if you want them.*

## Soft-handoff patterns

Default end state: a takeaway.

When a soft match is earned:

> If you want that kind of continuity day to day, [Product] is built for it. You’re also fine leaving with just this.

When no match:

> You don’t need another app for this. The useful idea is [takeaway].

Never:

- Make non-click a failure state
- Lead with product names before the insight
- Stack three product pitches in one reply

## Safety (Ask / agent)

- Never give medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations
- If someone shares personal health information, decline to engage with it and remind them not to share it here
- Stay grounded in [PRODUCTS.md](./PRODUCTS.md) — do not invent capabilities
- Label recorded / simulated sessions honestly when they are not live
