# Famile vision

## Purpose

**famile.xyz** holds attention before action — a field where stillness and measurement meet. Mira offers conversation. Sukari, Orbura, and Ardum hold longer practice arcs.

Copy should **embody** calm and trust. It should not narrate the UX or apologize for products.

## Tone

Minimal. Philosophical without sermon. Empirical without jargon walls.

East + West lightly: attention, stillness, return — alongside measure, evidence, adherence.

## Division of labor

| Layer | Owns |
|-------|------|
| **famile.xyz** | Atmosphere, Mira, quiet orientation |
| **Sukari / Orbura / Ardum** | Continuous practice and care |

## Mira

Company and clarity. No medical advice. No funnel endings.

## What we avoid on the site

- Meta manifesto (“you are the story”, “not a pitch”, “never the point”)
- Clinical “check in” tone
- Urgency or conversion theater
